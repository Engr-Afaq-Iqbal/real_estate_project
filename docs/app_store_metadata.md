## Full Description (≈ 500 words)

**BuildOS — The Complete Construction Manager for Homeowners, Contractors & Developers**

Whether you're building a 5-Marla house in Lahore, a commercial plaza in Dubai, or a residential complex in London, BuildOS puts every detail of your construction project in your pocket.

---

### 🏗️ Stage-by-Stage Progress Tracking

Break your project into stages — Foundation, Gray Structure, Plastering, Tiling, and more — and track each one with a visual progress bar. Mark stages complete, log delays (rain, Eid holiday, material shortage), and keep everyone updated in real time. No more calling your site supervisor five times a day.

### 💰 Budget That Doesn't Surprise You

Set your total budget, assign milestone amounts per stage, and track every rupee (or SAR, AED, GBP) spent. The budget-vs-actual breakdown per stage makes it instantly clear if a stage is going over budget before it's too late. Log material purchases, track thekedar (contractor) payments, and approve stage payment releases with a single tap.

### 👷 Labor Attendance & Payroll

Mark daily attendance with a single tap — Present, Absent, Half Day, Overtime, or Leave — for your entire team in seconds. The Sat–Thu work week is built in for Pakistan and Gulf markets. Generate weekly payroll summaries automatically. No spreadsheets, no paper registers.

### 📦 Material Purchase Log

Record every cement bag, steel rod, and tile delivery with supplier name, quantity, unit price, payment method, and optional receipt photo. The running material cost total keeps your procurement honest.

### 🧑‍💼 Team Management

Assign site supervisors, foremen, and QA inspectors to your project. Each supervisor can log updates, upload photos, and mark attendance from their own device. Verified construction companies show trust badges and star ratings so you always know who you're hiring.

### 🌍 Multi-Country, Multi-Currency

Instantly switch between Pakistan, Saudi Arabia, UAE, UK, India, and 20+ other markets. Prices, area units (Marla/Sq Ft/Sq m), work weeks (Sat–Thu / Sun–Thu / Mon–Fri), and construction rates all update automatically.

### 📄 Progress Reports & Sharing

Generate a professional progress report with one tap — share it via WhatsApp, email, or download as PDF. Keep your bank, architect, or family investor informed without any extra effort.

### 🔔 Smart Notifications

Get notified when a stage is updated, a payment is released, or attendance is overdue. All notifications are configurable from Settings.

---

**Built for Pakistan's construction market first — then adapted for the Gulf, Europe, and Southeast Asia.**

Whether you're an owner managing your dream home, a developer overseeing 10 projects, or a contractor building trust with clients — BuildOS is the one app that does it all.
